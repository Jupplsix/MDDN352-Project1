Nightly

Don't like walking alone at night?

Let Nightly Guide You.

What does it do?

Nightly aims to make traveling at night safer, by creating small walking groups that anyone can join.

Simply let Nightly know where you wanna go and it'll match you with a walking group going in the same direction.

Three Steps to Every Journey

Just takes a couple taps, and a few steps.

Follow the Blue

Nightly will guide you to the groups current location, after which everyone will walk until they reach their own split points.

Users will leave the group and continue to their destination.
